To connect your mobile as webcam:

Prerequisites
Android:
	1- dev47apps's Droidcam
	2- ShareIt Android. (Optinal)
Windows:
	1- dev47apps's Droidcam client
	2- ShareIt desktop. (Optinal)
	
	
Steps:
1- Connect your pc directly to mobile using shareIt (Faster),
	or make the mobile and pc in the same wirless network.
2- Open Droidcam on your mobile.
3- Open Droidcam client on your pc and use the Ip and port in the mobile to connect
